Detailed Description

Strong stability preserving Runge-Kutta 3 (SSP-RK3) method.

Constructor & Destructor Documentation

◆ FlowSolverRHEA() [1/2]

FlowSolverRHEA::FlowSolverRHEA ( )

Index of Riemann solver scheme.

Primitive variables:

  • Density: rho
  • Velocities: u, v, w
  • Specific total energy: E = e + ke ... sum of specific internal energy e, and specific kinetic energy ke = (u*u + v*v + w*w)/2 Conserved variables:
  • Mass: rho
  • Momentum: rho*u, rho*v, rho*w
  • Total energy: rho*E Thermodynamic state:
  • Pressure: P
  • Temperature: T
  • Speed of sound: sos
  • Specific gas constant: R_specific
  • Ratio of heat capacities: gamma Transport coefficients:
  • Dynamic viscosity: mu
  • Thermal conductivity: kappa
